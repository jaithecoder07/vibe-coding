Festival Highlights

Uttarayan

Gujarat

The sky comes alive with colorful kites as people compete in friendly battles.

Lohri

Punjab

Bonfires, bhangra, and popcorn mark the end of winter and the harvest of rabi crops.

Pongal

Tamil Nadu

Boiling fresh milk and rice in earthen pots to thank the Sun God.

Bihu

Assam

Traditional Bihu dance, feast, and buffalo fights mark the harvest cheer.
